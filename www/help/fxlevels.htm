FX Amounts

The kX Mixer FX (DSP effects) amounts page permits control of the overall FX levels.

Wave Stereo Reverb
This slider controls the Reverb amount for Wave playback.

Wave Stereo Chorus
This slider controls the Chorus amount for Wave playback.

Refer to the kX DSP guide and to the kX Router guide for more details on FXBus usage and default mappings.
