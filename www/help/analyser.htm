Analyser

kX Mixer includes an Analyser - a graphic representation of the Wave and Synth voices, displayed according to their volume.

Synth voices are displayed in blue, Wave voices are displayed in red and ASIO voices are displayed in green.

Volume is displayed lighter (blue - light blue, green - light green, red - orange).

To acess the Peak Meter(s), use kX DSP applet or Effects submenu.
